https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/pub/data/swdi/stormevents/csvfiles

The downloaded reports are saved as: 
StormEvents_details-ftp_v1.0_d1950_[mod_date].csv" to "StormEvents_details-ftp_v1.0_d2022_[mod_date].csv
in:
Data/Raw/OBS/NOAA_Rep
